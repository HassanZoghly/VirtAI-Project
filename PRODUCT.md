# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Educational institutions, educators, and school administrators looking for secure, curriculum-aware AI teaching assistants. They operate in highly regulated environments where trust, data privacy, and academic rigor are paramount.

## Product Purpose
Provide a secure, privacy-first, curriculum-aware virtual AI teaching assistant platform. Success means helping schools seamlessly deploy AI TAs that enhance student engagement and academic outcomes through low-latency voice, curricular alignment, and clean interactive elements.

## Brand Personality
Professional, academic, trusted. 3-word personality: *Rigorous, Premium, Authoritative*. It should feel like a top-tier educational institution's bespoke tool.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS templates: overused tracked uppercase kickers above every section, identical multi-card grids with big colorful icons, and bloated feature sections.
- Cheap styling indicators: ghost cards (borders combined with wide drop shadows), overly rounded elements (radii > 16px), and random decorative graphics or sketches.
- Overstimulating visual noise: unnecessary parallax, diagonal stripes, or flashing elements.

## Design Principles
1. **Academic Gravitas**: Design for high trust and structural integrity. Every element should feel deliberate and serious.
2. **Clear Layout Rhythm**: Use varied spacing and structural grids over lazy card containment. Let the typography and content drive the layout.
3. **Immersive Performance**: Emphasize the platform's advanced technology (low-latency, real-time pipelines) through visual speed, smoothness, and immediate responsiveness.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target: WCAG 2.2 AA compliance.
- Contrast: Strict minimum 4.5:1 text-to-background ratio (and 3:1 for large text).
- Keyboard: Fully navigable, clear outline states.
- Motion: Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` with clean, fallback layouts.
